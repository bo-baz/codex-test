Our Collaborative Process

Plan Your Session

Share your AI-generated track, your vision for live instrumentation, and any reference material. We schedule time with the right musicians and engineers to match your sound.

Record & Refine

Join us in the studio or dial in virtually while we capture live takes, layer harmonies, and refine arrangements. You get real-time feedback and unlimited revisions during your booked session.

Finalize & Protect

Once the mix is locked, we master the track, package final deliverables, and walk you through copyright registration and digital distribution submission.
